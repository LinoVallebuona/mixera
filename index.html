From a Passion Project to a Mission.

Founded by a 17-year-old student in Germany.

MIXERA was born from a simple idea: what if professional-grade DJ equipment was actually affordable? My name is Lino, and as a student and aspiring DJ, I was frustrated by the high prices of top-tier controllers. The gear with the best features always seemed just out of reach.

I decided to change that. I started designing and prototyping in my spare time, driven by a passion for both music and technology. My goal was to build a controller that I would want to useâ€”one with no compromises on features, build quality, or feel.

This GoFundMe campaign is the next step in making that vision a reality. The funds will go directly into producing the first batch of controllers, all of which will be carefully assembled and tested right here in Germany. Thank you for believing in this project and helping to empower the next generation of DJs.
